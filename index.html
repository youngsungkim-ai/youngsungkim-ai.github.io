Research Group

We focus on fundamental studies in the field of Artificial Intelligence. More specifically, we currently focus on deep learning based AI for perception (recognition), reasoning, and generation purposes.

Join Our Group!

We are continually seeking highly motivated students at both undergraduate and graduate levels for full-time and part-time positions. Compensation will be based on the applicant's skill set and contributions. Priority areas include Neurosymbolic (Logical) Neural Networks and Multimodal Deep Models. The application can be sent to y.kim AT inha.ac.kr.
